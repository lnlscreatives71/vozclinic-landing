2. Personal data we collect

We collect personal data directly from you when you complete a form on our site (waitlist, book a demo), contact us by email or WhatsApp, or engage our services. Depending on the case, this may include:

  • Identification and contact data: first and last name, email address, phone / WhatsApp number.
  • Clinic data: clinic name, city, clinic type, management system, message volume, and other operational information you share with us.
  • Service-usage data: messages and requests that our AI receptionist (Sofía) handles over WhatsApp on behalf of your clinic, including patient data for scheduling purposes (name, contact, reason for the appointment).

We do not intentionally collect special categories of sensitive data through the site. When our service processes patient data on behalf of a clinic, we act as a processor, under that clinic’s instructions and privacy notice.

3. Purposes of processing

Primary purposes (necessary)

  • Respond to your requests for information, demos, and support.
  • Manage the waitlist and let you know when the service is available for your clinic.
  • Provide the bilingual AI receptionist service: answer messages, book appointments, and follow up over WhatsApp.
  • Billing, compliance with legal obligations, and administration of the contractual relationship.

Secondary purposes (optional)

  • Send you marketing communications, product news, and related content.
  • Produce statistics and improve our services.

If you do not want your data processed for the secondary purposes, you may say so at any time by writing to hola@vozclinic.com. Your refusal will not be grounds to deny you the services you request.

4. Transfers and processors

To operate, we rely on providers that process data on our behalf (processors), such as WhatsApp messaging services (Meta Platforms), cloud hosting and infrastructure, transactional email, and calendar and CRM tools. Some of these providers may be located in the United States or other countries, so your data may be processed outside Mexico under contracts requiring confidentiality and equivalent security measures.

We do not transfer your personal data to third parties for purposes other than those described here without your consent, except in the cases provided for in Article 37 of the LFPDPPP (for example, requirements from a competent authority).

5. Text messaging (SMS)

If you provide a mobile phone number and opt in, VozClinic and the clinics we serve may send you text messages (SMS) related to the service — for example, appointment confirmations, reminders, and replies to questions or requests you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or your SMS opt-in consent with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Your mobile information is used only to provide the messaging service you requested, and is disclosed only to the service providers that help us deliver those messages (such as our messaging and telephony platform), under confidentiality obligations.

Message frequency varies and depends on how you interact with the service — for example, the appointments you schedule and the questions you ask. Message and data rates may apply, according to your mobile carrier and plan.

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message; reply HELP for help. You may also contact us at hola@vozclinic.com.

6. Cross-border patients

When data of patients from the United States is processed, we seek to comply with applicable Mexican regulations and to honor the confidentiality commitments inherent to health information. The clinic using VozClinic is responsible for obtaining its patients’ consent under its own privacy notice.

7. Your ARCO rights

You have the right to Access your personal data, Rectify it when inaccurate, Cancel it when you believe it is not needed, and Object to its processing (ARCO rights). You may also revoke your consent or limit the use or disclosure of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, send a request to hola@vozclinic.com including:

  • Your name and a means of contact for our response.
  • A copy of an official ID proving your identity (or legal representation, where applicable).
  • A clear description of the data and the right you wish to exercise.

We will respond within the timeframes set by the LFPDPPP.

8. Revoking consent

You may revoke the consent you have given us to process your data at any time by writing to hola@vozclinic.com. In some cases, revocation may mean we can no longer provide the service to you.

9. Cookies and tracking technologies

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, measure site usage, and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may be affected.

10. Security measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data against damage, loss, alteration, destruction, or unauthorized use, access, or processing.

11. Changes to this Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable law. The current version will always be available at vozclinic.com/privacy, with its last-updated date.
