Who's a good fit to refer

Yes — Full Integration fit (Design Partner eligible):

  • Dental clinics in Tijuana, Rosarito, or Ensenada
  • Run their calendar in Google Calendar
  • Or use any PMS with an available API — Doctocliq, Nimbo, Dentalink, DenPro, Adstra, Curve, Open Dental, and others
  • Or run Doctocliq with active sync to Google Calendar
  • Or use paper, Excel, or WhatsApp directly to book
  • More than 100 WhatsApp messages per week
  • Bilingual patient base (cross-border or locally mixed)
  • Med spas count too — same profile applies

Also fit — Comms-Only (standard $299/mo from day one, no Design Partner):

  • · Clinics on any PMS that has no API available today
  • · Sofía handles WhatsApp and inbox; front desk books manually
  • · When API key becomes available, live scheduling activates with no price change

Not a fit:

  • · Clinics that decline both Full Integration and Comms-Only
  • · Clinics with fully staffed 24/7 reception and no unattended WhatsApp volume
  • · Clinics outside Baja California (for now)
If you're not sure about their PMS, refer them anyway — I'll figure it out in the first conversation.

Frequently asked questions

What if the clinic I refer is Comms-Only (PMS without API)?
That still counts as Phase 1 fit. The clinic starts on the Comms-Only plan — Sofía handles WhatsApp and the inbox, front desk books manually, same Esencial $299/mo price. Your 3-month bonus activates the same way when that clinic becomes an active partner (signs + completes onboarding). Comms-Only doesn’t get Design Partner terms (90 days free + 50% lifetime), but it does count for your referral bonus.
What if the clinic declines both Full Integration and Comms-Only?
Then I add them to the waitlist. Your 3-month bonus only activates when a clinic becomes an active founding partner (signs + completes onboarding). I’m still hugely grateful for the intro.
Is there a limit on how many clinics I can refer?
No. Every clinic that becomes a founding partner through you adds 3 more months. If you bring 4 active clinics, that's 12 free months from the day we integrate your PMS.
When do the 3 months get counted?
The months are added starting the day you activate your own 90-day pilot (when we integrate your PMS). If your normal plan would be 90 days free + paid after, the 3 extra months apply after your original free period.
Can referrals be outside Tijuana?
For now we're focused on Baja California. If you know a clinic in CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey or another city, send it anyway — I'll add them to a Phase 3 list and let you know if we open that city. But the 3-month bonus only applies to Phase 1 fits in BC right now.
What if the clinic already knew me and contacted me directly?
If the clinic mentions you or shows they came through your intro (screenshot, forwarded email, etc.) and completes onboarding, I add the 3 months. If they reach out independently without mentioning you, I don't. Best practice: send me the intro before they reach out on their own.
